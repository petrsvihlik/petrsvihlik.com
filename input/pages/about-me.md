My name is Petr Švihlík but people often call me Rocky. Don't ask [why](/assets/img/rocky-why.jpg) 😂

My work is usually related to open-source software, Developer Experience, Developer Relations, and empowering developers in general. Currently, I work as a VP of Engineering for Kentico. Before that, I worked in a Developer Success team for Microsoft's Azure Communication Services.

Outside of work, I enjoy [music](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mike_Oldfield), driving my car, running, and above all [traveling the world with my girlfriend](/assets/img/travelling-with-girlfriend.png). Lately, these activities have been hindered by a new family member 😈 who tries to steal all that's left of our collective attention.

When you check out my [projects](/pages/projects) on GitHub, you'll soon learn that most of them are somehow related to .NET. That's a correct observation, I love spending my time trying out new C# language features and exploring .NET Foundation projects. This project is no exception and it's a part of my learning process. This time I tried to pick up [Statiq](https://statiq.dev/)—a static site generator for .NET.
